## Notes

- These are simple 120 mm DC brushless case fans, so a separate installation manual may not exist outside the host case/manual bundle.
- The SilverStone-branded CC12025L12S label in the photo lists 0.07A. The linked OEM fan-family datasheet may contain multiple current/speed variants, so prefer the photographed label for this exact installed fan.
- Before powering the rebuilt system, connect chassis fans to appropriate motherboard SYS_FAN headers or a known-good fan hub, then verify spin and BIOS RPM readings.
